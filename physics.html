Physics Concept Bank

1. Kinematics (Motion)
v = u + at
s = ut + ½at²
v² = u² + 2as

Example: If a car starts from rest (u=0) and accelerates at 2m/s² for 10s, its final velocity v = 0 + (2*10) = 20m/s.

2. Work, Energy & Power
Work (W) = F × d × cos(θ)
Kinetic Energy = ½mv²
Potential Energy = mgh

Example: A 2kg object at 5m height has P.E = 2 * 9.8 * 5 = 98 Joules.
