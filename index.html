Introduction

JEE Mains sirf ek exam nahi hai, ye lakho students ke sapno ki udaan hai. Har saal 12 lakh se zyada bacche isme baithte hain, lekin sirf wahi top par pahunchte hain jinki strategy 'Bulletproof' hoti hai...

Subject-Wise Deep Dive

Physics: Mechanics se lekar Modern Physics tak, har chapter ka apna ek weightage hai. Aapko HC Verma ke har ek sawal ko at least 2 baar solve karna chahiye. Concepts ko visualize karne ke liye animation videos ka sahara lein.

Chemistry: Ye rank-maker subject hai. Inorganic Chemistry ke liye NCERT ki har ek line 'Gita' ki tarah hai. Organic mein reactions ko ratne ke bajaye mechanisms par dhyan dein. Physical Chemistry mein calculation speed badhayein.

Mathematics: Calculus aur Algebra ko ignore karna matlab rank se hath dhona. Rozana 50 sawal practice karne ka sankalp lein...
