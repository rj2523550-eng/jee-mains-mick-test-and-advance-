Important Strategy Tip

Agar aap JEE Mains ki taiyari kar rahe hain, toh hamesha yaad rakhein ki consistency hi sabse badi power hai. Niche diye gaye points ko follow karein:

  • Daily revision karna zaroori hai.
  • Mock tests ko ignore na karein.
  • NCERT Chemistry ki har line ko dhyan se padhein.

Aap is file mein jitna chahein lamba blog likh sakte hain, ye card apne aap niche bada hota jayega.
