1. Syllabus Ko Priority Ke Hisaab Se Baantein

Sabse pehle un chapters ko pakdein jo exam mein sabse zyada marks dilate hain. Physics mein Modern Physics aur Optics, Maths mein Calculus aur Vectors, aur Chemistry mein Organic Chemistry (GOC) aur Chemical Bonding sabse zaruri hain.

2. Daily Routine Aur Consistency

JEE crack karne ke liye din mein 12 ghante padhna zaruri nahi hai, balki 6-8 ghante rozana (consistently) padhna zaruri hai. Har subject ko barabar waqt dein. Physics ke liye numericals, Chemistry ke liye NCERT reading, aur Maths ke liye zyada se zyada practice par dhyan dein.

Pro Tip: Rozana kam se kam 20-30 PYQs (Previous Year Questions) solve karein. Isse aapko exam ke pattern ka asli andaza lagega.

3. NCERT: Chemistry Ka Brahmastra

Inorganic aur Physical Chemistry ke liye NCERT ki ek-ek line zaruri hai. JEE Mains ke paper mein aksar sawal seedha NCERT se utha liye jaate hain. Isliye koi bhi badi reference book uthane se pehle NCERT ko 3-4 baar revice zarur karein.

4. Mock Tests Aur Self-Analysis

Hamari website JEE Master Hub par humne special free mock tests design kiye hain. Yaad rakhiye, test dena utna zaruri nahi hai jitna us test ko analyze karna hai. Dekhiye ki aapne kahan galti ki—kya wo calculation error tha ya concept ki kami thi?

5. Revision Ki Best Technique

Haar hafte ke ant mein (Sunday ko) naya kuch na padhein. Sirf purane notes aur formulae ko revise karein. Short notes banaiye taaki exam se 10 din pehle aap puri Physics sirf 2 ghante mein revise kar sakein.

Conclusion: JEE Mains 2026 ek marathon hai, sprint nahi. Khud par bharosa rakhein aur roz thoda-thoda progress karte rahein. Hum aapke saath hain har step par!
