JEE Mains 2026 Result & JEE Advanced Dates: Sab Kuch Jo Aapko Janna Zaroori Hai

JEE Mains 2026 Session 1 ki parikshaen samapt ho chuki hain aur ab lakhon aspirants ko besabri se apne result ka intezar hai. National Testing Agency (NTA) har saal ki tarah is saal bhi pariksha ke kuch hi dinon baad result aur answer key jari karne ki taiyari mein hai. Is blog mein hum vistar se charcha karenge ki JEE Mains Result Date, Session 2 Registration, aur JEE Advanced 2026 ki mahatvapurn tithiyan (dates) kya ho sakti hain.

1. JEE Mains 2026 Session 1 Result Date

NTA ke pichle kuch saalon ke record ko dekha jaye toh pariksha ke antim din ke lagbhag 10 se 12 dinon ke andar result jari kar diya jata hai. Session 1 ki parikshaen January ke antim saptah mein sampann hui hain.

Expected Date: Ummid ki ja rahi hai ki JEE Mains Session 1 ka result **February 12 se February 15, 2026** ke beech official website (jeemain.nta.nic.in) par jari kar diya jayega. Result ke saath hi NTA final answer key aur toppers ki list bhi jari karega. Candidates ko apna Application Number aur Date of Birth taiyar rakhna chahiye.

2. JEE Mains Session 2 Registration Details

Jo students Session 1 mein accha perform nahi kar paaye ya jo apna score improve karna chahte hain, unke liye Session 2 ek sunhera mauka hota hai. Session 2 ki registration prakriya Session 1 ke result ke aas-paas hi shuru ho jati hai.

3. JEE Advanced 2026 Exam Date & Eligibility

JEE Mains ke top 2,50,000 qualifiers ko JEE Advanced mein baithne ka mauka milta hai, jo IITs mein admission ka rasta hai. Is saal JEE Advanced ke liye tithiyan pehle se hi prastavit hain.

Exam Date: JEE Advanced 2026 ki pariksha **May 24, 2026 (Sunday)** ko hone ki puri sambhavna hai. Iska registration April ke antim saptah mein shuru hoga jab JEE Mains ka final rank card jari ho jayega.

4. Agle Kuch Mahinon Ki Strategy

Ab jab result aur registration ka samay kareeb hai, toh students ko apni strategy badalni hogi. Physics mein formula revision aur derivation par focus karein. Chemistry mein NCERT ki har ek line Session 2 ke liye rank-booster sabit ho sakti hai. Mathematics mein calculus aur vectors ki daily practice karein.

Mock tests dena band na karein. Har hafte kam se kam 2 full-length tests dein aur unka bariki se analysis karein. Yaad rakhiye, JEE sirf knowledge ka exam nahi hai, ye aapke Time Management aur Pressure Handling ki bhi pariksha hai.

5. Savdhani aur Official Websites

Internet par bahut sari fake news aur unofficial dates ghumti rehti hain. Hum apne pathakon (readers) ko salah dete hain ki hamesha niche di gayi official websites par hi bharosa karein:

  • jeeadv.ac.in (Advanced ke liye)
  • nta.ac.in (Official Notifications ke liye)

Bhaiyon, 2026 ka saal aapki mehnat ka fal dene wala saal hai. Agar Session 1 accha nahi gaya, toh Session 2 abhi baaki hai. Himmat na hare aur apni taiyari jaari rakhein. Agle sabhi updates ke liye hamare portal se jude rahein!
