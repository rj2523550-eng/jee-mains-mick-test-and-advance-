1. The Syllabus Revolution: Will it Change?

Pichle kuch saalon mein NTA ne JEE Mains ke syllabus ko NCERT ke rationalized syllabus ke saath align karne ki koshish ki hai. 2027 tak, yeh ummeed hai ki syllabus aur bhi streamlined hoga.

  • Physics: Experimental skills par zyada focus diya ja raha hai. Vernier Callipers, Screw Gauge jaise topics ab mandatory part hain.
  • Chemistry: Inorganic Chemistry mein p-block jaise bade chapters ko simplify kiya gaya hai, lekin unke reactions abhi bhi critical hain.
  • Mathematics: Calculus aur Vectors mein conceptual depth badhayi ja rahi hai.

2. Exam Pattern and Section B Changes

NTA ne Section B (Numerical Value Questions) mein options (10 mein se 5 karne ka mauka) khatam karne ka trend shuru kiya hai.
Prediction for 2027: Ho sakta hai ki Section B mein har sawal compulsory ho jaye ya negative marking ka criteria aur sakht kar diya jaye. JEE 2027 aspirants ko bina options ke taiyari karne ki aadat dalni chahiye.

3. Number of Attempts: The Big Question

Filhal JEE Mains saal mein do baar (January aur April) hota hai. Students ke beech aksar charcha rehti hai ki kya attempts badhenge ya ghaatenge.

2027 ke liye hamari salah hai ki aap **January Session** ko hi apna final attempt maan kar chalein. Aksar dekha gaya hai ki January session mein kam marks par zyada percentile milti hai kyunki April tak sabhi bacchon ki taiyari poori ho jati hai aur competition badh jata hai.

4. Normalization and Percentile System

Kyunki exam kai shifts mein hota hai, NTA "Normalization" process ka use karti hai. Iska matlab hai ki agar aapki shift ka paper tough aaya hai, toh kam marks par bhi aapki percentile achi banegi. 2027 ke bacchon ko "Raw Score" ke bajaye "Percentile" ka logic samajhna hoga. 5. Digitalization and AI Monitoring

NTA ab cheaters ko pakadne ke liye AI (Artificial Intelligence) aur biometric monitoring ko aur advance kar raha hai.

  • Adhaar Authentication: Exam form bharte waqt Adhaar verification compulsory hai.
  • CCTV Surveillance: Live monitoring ke zariye anuchit sadhanon (unfair means) par sakht karwayi hoti hai.
