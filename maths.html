1. Quadratic Equations
Standard Form: ax² + bx + c = 0
Roots (x) = [-b ± √(b² - 4ac)] / 2a
Sum of roots (α+β) = -b/a
Product of roots (αβ) = c/a

Quick Example: For x² - 5x + 6 = 0, roots are 2 and 3. Sum = 5, Product = 6.

2. Trigonometry Identities
sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
sin(2θ) = 2sinθcosθ
cos(2θ) = cos²θ - sin²θ = 2cos²θ - 1
Mind Map: Sin → Cos → Tan transformations are key for Calculus.
3. Differentiation (Calculus)
d/dx (xⁿ) = nxⁿ⁻¹
d/dx (sin x) = cos x
d/dx (log x) = 1/x
Product Rule: (uv)' = u'v + uv'

Example: d/dx (x³) = 3x².
